🍜 ShinShin Hakata DEITOS — First Ramen in Japan
Inside Hakata Station — 2 min from airport. Fukuoka's most-loved local ramen. Light yet rich tonkotsu broth with no heavy pork smell, thin noodles (order firm/kata), silky chashu. Also try the yakimeshi fried rice. ~¥900. Open daily until 11pm — Wednesday branch is open when Tenjin branch is not.
📍 Map
17:15
⛩️ Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine
One of Kyushu's most important Shinto shrines. Ancient camphor trees, peak cherry blossoms. Try umegaemochi rice cakes. Free entry. Open until 6:30pm — arriving at 17:15 gives ~75 mins, plenty of time. Flat paved path throughout.

📍 Thursday 10 April
8:30
🚗 Depart Tosu → Fukuoka
~40 min drive north to Ohori Park.
9:30
🌸 Ohori Park
Peak cherry blossom — flat 2km lake loop, swan boats, Kusama pumpkin. Starbucks lakeside. Free entry.
📍 Map
11:30
🐟 Lunch — Kurodahan, Daimyo
4.9★ from nearly 2,000 reviews — one of Fukuoka's most celebrated seafood rice bowl restaurants. Signature bluefin tuna bowl and otoro melt in the mouth. Mixed maguro bowl highly recommended. ~¥2,000–3,000/person. 10 min walk from Ohori Park. Thursday ✅ (closed Mon & Tue). ⚠️ Lunch booking n/a, expect to queue.
📍 Map
13:00
🤖 Gundam Park / LaLaport Fukuoka
Life-size Gundam — light/sound show on the hour outside. Japanese retail, browse inside. Validated parking.
📍 Map
15:30
🛁 Minato Onsen Namiha no Yu 
Best public onsen in Fukuoka city. Multiple pools, sauna, bay night views. ~¥1,250. Free parking with validation. Thursday = ladies discount. 🔴 Tattoos NOT allowed in public baths — 5 private baths (kashikiri) available at front counter for tattooed guests. 💡 Tattoo-friendly alternative nearby: Azumayu Sento (1 Chome-29-3 Chiyo, Hakata Ward) — classic neighbourhood bathhouse, known to accept tattooed guests, very local, very cheap ~¥500.
📍 Map
17:30
🚗 Drive back to Tosu — Park Car
~40 min. Park car at accommodation. Walk/taxi to Tosu Station. JR Kagoshima Main Line → Hakata ~30 min, ~¥500/person. Check last train time before going (~midnight).
18:30
🏮 Nakasu Yatai — Arrive at 6pm Opening
15 min walk from Hakata Station. Mentaiko, yakitori, ramen, highball. Everyone drinks freely — no driving tonight. ~¥3,000–4,000/person.
📍 Map
20:30
🍢 Nakasu Oden (Late Night)
Only 11 seats, run by a lovely old couple. Deep umami broth, cabbage rolls, sake. Open until 1am. Korean/Japanese menu. May need to wait — worth it. JR back to Tosu before midnight.

03
Friday 11 April
Itoshima Coast — Keya Sea Cave, Oyster BBQ, Low Tide Torii & Seaside Coffee
▼
📍 Friday 11 April
8:30
🚗 Depart Tosu → Itoshima, Keya Coast
~1 hr drive northwest. Head directly to Keya Port area — Shirahamaya and Keya no Oto sea cave are both here. Low tide forecast ~2pm — plan to be at the Torii by 1:45pm for best access.
9:30
🌊 Keya no Oto Sea Cave
One of Japan's largest sea caves — dramatic lava rock formations carved by waves. Flat coastal park walks and viewpoints. Optional boat ride into the cave (small fee, weather dependent). Free entry to park. Open 9am–4:30pm. Right next to Shirahamaya — perfect way to fill time before lunch.
📍 Map
11:30
🦪 Lunch — Hamayaki Shirahamaya, Keya Port
Best oyster BBQ in Itoshima — stylish open-air ocean terrace right at the water's edge. Gas-fired roasters (no smoke smell on hair or clothes). Live seafood tank — fresh Itoshima oysters, scallops, squid from Yobuko, Goto prawns. English menu. ~¥3,000–5,000/person. Open until 9pm. ⚠️ Book ahead: +81 92-328-3282
📍 Map
13:30
🚗 Drive Keya → Futamigaura
~15 min drive north along the coast. Arrive just before low tide at 2pm.
13:45
⛩️ Sakurai Futamigaura Couple Stones & Torii
Sacred rocks fully exposed, best access and photos of the trip. Sacred rocks joined by shimenawa rope, iconic floating torii gate in the sea. Free entry.
📍 Map
15:00
☕ Beach Cafe SUNSET — Ocean View Coffee
5 min drive from Torii. Ocean terrace, watch the sea. Open until 8pm. Friday ✅ (closed Thursdays).
📍 Map
16:00
🚗 Drive back toward Tosu via Kurume
~1 hr drive southeast. Stop at Yunosakakurume on the way.
17:30
🛁 Yunosakakurume Hot Spring
Authentic local onsen, 30 min south of Tosu. Outdoor garden bath with waterfall, indoor pools, sauna. ~¥880. Open until 1am. Very local crowd, excellent silky mineral water. 🔴 Tattoo policy unconfirmed — as a traditional local onsen likely restricted. Call ahead (+81 942-33-4126) or use the tattoo-friendly alternative below. 💡 Tattoo-friendly alternative: Izuminoyu Kurume (25 min from Tosu, private group pool, ✅ tattoo friendly, open until 3am, ~¥1,200/group) — use this instead if tattooed guests are turned away.
📍 Map
19:30
🍶 Dinner — Neighbourhood Izakaya near Tosu
Fresh sashimi, yakitori, cold beer. ~¥3,000/person.

▼
📍 Saturday 12 April
8:30
🚗 Depart Tosu → Nagasaki
~1.5 hr drive west on expressway.
10:00
🕊️ Hypocenter Park + Atomic Bomb Museum
Atomic bomb ground zero — free entry to park. ¥200 museum. Combine with Peace Park (5 min walk). Deeply moving. Allow 1–1.5 hrs.
📍 Map
11:30
🐟 Lunch — Koda Shokudo (Fishmonger Restaurant)
Run directly by a fishmonger — exceptional freshness. Sashimi set meal from ¥1,500. Famous for Nagasaki aji (horse mackerel) and fried whiting. Lunch only 11:30am–2pm. Saturday ✅ (Closed Sundays).
📍 Map
13:30
🏯 Glover Garden
Historic Western-style mansion gardens on a hillside — sweeping harbour views. ¥620 entry. Moving walkways going up — no strenuous walking. April cherry blossoms.
15:00
🚶 Explore Nagasaki Old Town
Dejima Dutch trading post, local coffee, easy walk before the night viewing.
18:30
🌃 Inasa-yama Night View
One of Japan's top 3 night views — Nagasaki harbour from 333m. Ropeway ¥500 return. Go at dusk to catch golden hour + night panorama. Restaurant at top.
📍 Map
20:30
🚗 Drive Nagasaki → Tosu
~1.5 hrs. Arrive ~10pm.
22:30
🛁 Izuminoyu Kurume
Private group onsen pool, open until 3am. ~¥1,200/group. Tattoo friendly. Perfect after a long day. Book ahead if possible.

05
Sunday 13 April
Karatsu Castle Town + Yanagawa Canal Boat — Easy Sunday
▼
📍 Sunday 13 April
9:00
🚗 Depart Tosu → Karatsu
~1 hr drive west on Route 263.
9:30
🏯 Karatsu Castle
Coastal castle on a headland above the sea — ¥200, panoramic sea views, elevator available. 200-year-old wisteria tree in full April bloom at the entrance. Browse Karatsu-yaki pottery shops along the approach — unique, lightweight gifts.
📍 Map
11:30
🦑 Lunch — Fresh Ika (Squid) Sashimi, Karatsu Harbour
Karatsu is famous for ultra-fresh squid sashimi. Find a local restaurant near the castle or harbour. Search イカ料理 (ika ryori) on Google Maps. ~¥1,500–2,500/person.
13:00
🚗 Drive Karatsu → Yanagawa
~1 hr south. Arrive by 2pm for the canal boat.
14:00
🚣 Yanagawa Canal Boat — Donko-bune
70-min flat-bottomed boat punted through willow-lined canals — ~¥2,000/person. Boatman sings traditional songs. No reservation needed — boats leave when full. Last boat 3pm, arrive by 2pm. Free shuttle bus from Yanagawa Station.
📍 Map
16:00
🍱 Early Dinner — Yanagawa Unaju (Grilled Eel over Rice)
Yanagawa's signature dish — rich, smoky, deeply satisfying. Find a riverside restaurant after the boat. Drive back to Tosu after (~30 min), home by ~5:30pm.
18:30
🛁 筑紫野温泉 アマンディ 
Second visit — on the way home from Yanagawa. Bali-inspired garden onsen, private rooms available. ~¥800. Open until midnight. 🔴 Tattoo policy unconfirmed — if tattooed, ask for a kashikiri (private room) at the counter on arrival. 💡 Alternative: Izuminoyu Kurume — confirmed tattoo friendly, private group pool ~¥1,200/group, 20 min from Tosu.
📍 Map
Estimated daily spend (excl. car/accommodation)
~¥8,000–10,000/person

Monday 14 April
Beppu Hell Springs → Hyotan Onsen (Michelin ★★★) → Yufuin Stroll
▼
📍 Monday 14 April
8:00
🚗 Depart Tosu → Beppu
~1.5 hr drive south on Kyushu Expressway.
9:30
🌋 Jigoku Meguri — Hell Springs Tour
8 volcanic hell pools: cobalt blue (Umi Jigoku), blood red (Chinoike), boiling grey mud (Oniyama), crocodile farm and more. ~¥2,200 combined ticket. All flat paths. Allow 1.5–2 hrs.
11:30
🍱 Lunch — Restaurant Yurari at Hyotan Onsen
Ryukyu raw fish rice bowl ¥1,300 (local Oita marinated fresh fish over rice — recommended). Or jigoku steam kitchen: cook your own crab legs over live geothermal steam. Oita beef bowl ¥1,200 also excellent.
📍 Map
12:30
🛁 Hyotan Onsen Public Baths — ⭐ Michelin 3-Star
✅ Fully tattoo friendly — all communal baths open to tattooed guests, verified March 2026. Split by gender — both sides equally impressive.

♀️ Women: Open-air baths, indoor baths, waterfall bath (takiyu), steam bath, iconic gourd-shaped hyotan tub
♂️ Men: 9 different baths — indoor, outdoor, sand bath, waterfall, steam room, cold plunge

Entry: ¥1,020/person. Sand bath: +¥760 (yukata rental included, buried in hot spring-heated sand — no time limit, deeply relaxing). Allow 1.5 hrs minimum.

After bathing: regroup in shared tatami rest area.
14:15
🍮 Onsen Steam Pudding + 🍦 Kabosu Soft Serve
Steam pudding made fresh daily with hot spring steam ~¥400. Then the famous kabosu (local citrus) soft serve ice cream ~¥400. Both sold at Hyotan — don't leave without trying both.
15:00
🚗 Drive Beppu → Yufuin
~30 min drive through mountain scenery.
15:30
🏞️ Lake Kinrin, Yufuin
Hot spring-fed lake — misty, serene, Mt Yufu backdrop. Flat shore path (10 mins). Ukiha Shrine on the water's edge. Beautiful late afternoon light.
16:15
🛍️ Yunotsubo Kaido Street, Yufuin
Wagyu beef skewer on a stick, tofu soft serve, local craft shops and small cafes. Very flat and pleasant. Drive back to Tosu after (~2 hrs, home ~7:30pm).
19:30
🍶 Dinner — Robata Grill or Seafood Izakaya near Tosu
Well-deserved after the trip's best day. Robata-grilled seafood, sake. ~¥3,000–4,000/person.
Note: Hyotan Onsen is open 9am–midnight — no booking required for public baths, just arrive. Budget ~3 hrs at Hyotan total (lunch + baths + post-soak relaxation).
Estimated daily spend (excl. car/accommodation)
